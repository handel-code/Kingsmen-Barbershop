About Us

The story behind Nairobi's most trusted barbershop.

Our Story

Kingsmen Barbershop was founded in 2018 by Marcus Odhiambo, a master barber with over 15 years of experience in the craft. What started as a small two-chair shop in Westlands has grown into one of Nairobi's most recognized grooming destinations.

Marcus built Kingsmen on a simple belief: every man deserves a great haircut delivered with respect, skill, and consistency. That philosophy has never changed, even as the shop has grown.

Today, Kingsmen operates with a team of six dedicated barbers, each trained in both classic and contemporary techniques. We serve clients from across Nairobi — from students to CEOs — and take pride in making every single one of them feel at home.
